3 Convenient Ways to Submit a Referral

Online Submission

  1. Fill Out the Form: Complete the referral form through our DocuSign Portal.
  2. Authorize Provider's Signature:
    • Sign the form yourself while completing it, or
    • Choose "Finish Later" and enter the provider's email to sign and finalize the order remotely.
  3. Attach Documents: Include all necessary supplementary documents.
  4. Submit the Form: Click "Finish" to send the completed order to Real Infusions.

Email Submission

  1. Complete the Form: Fill out the referral form using our DocuSign portal.
    • Afterward, download or print the form and secure the provider's signature.
    • Alternatively, print the order form directly from DocuSign and fill it out manually, ensuring to obtain the provider's signature.
  2. Attach Documents: Include the completed referral form and all required supplementary documents.
  3. Email the Documents: Send all documents via email to intake@realinfusions.com.

Fax Submission

  1. Complete the Form: Fill out the referral form using our DocuSign portal.
    • Afterward, download or print the form and secure the provider's signature.
    • Alternatively, print the order form directly from DocuSign and fill it out manually, ensuring to obtain the provider's signature.
  2. Attach Documents: Include the completed referral form and all required supplementary documents.
  3. Fax the Documents: Send all documents via fax to (305) 424-7240.
